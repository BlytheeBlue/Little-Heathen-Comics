About Us

Little Heathen Comics is a self-owned online comic company based out of Pueblo,
Colorado. Starting out with just bi-weekly postings of side-stories of the characters
of "Go to H" growing in popularity.....more information on the company to be added.

What We Do

As our company has expanded, so has our hearts. Year round, we sponsor family
events that raise money for children's charities, as well as hold food drives for
those in need. We hold high values to everyone in our community, and, as we love to
say, creativity is what drives the soul, so it's best not to lose it!

Since more positions have opened up, we've even been extending out into the
community to allow some of our more eager artists, writers, and other talented friends
join us in creating future pieces! We offer a few of the following:

  • Work Studies
  • Summer Internships
  • Art Workshops: for children and adults alike!
  • Possiblity to be hired on!
